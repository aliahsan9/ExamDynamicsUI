IELTS is a standardized test designed to assess the English language proficiency of non-native English speakers. It is jointly managed by the British Council, IDP: IELTS Australia, and Cambridge Assessment English.

  • Accepted by 11,000+ organizations worldwide.
  • Required for study, work, and immigration purposes.
  • Available in Academic and General Training formats.
